Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is available from 3:00 PM, and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged upon request, subject to availability.

Is parking available?

Yes, all our residences offer complimentary parking. The Classic and Mini residences have street parking available, while Village includes a private parking space.

Are pets allowed?

We welcome pets at our Village residence. Please contact us in advance to discuss arrangements. A small pet fee may apply.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 14 days or more before arrival receive a full refund. Cancellations within 14 days are subject to a one-night charge. No-shows are charged the full amount.
