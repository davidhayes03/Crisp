About Us

Specialist Healthcare Interiors

Established in August 2012, Crisp Interiors Ltd has been providing specialist interior refurbishment services for private hospitals across the UK.

We work directly with Ramsay Healthcare Group, one of the UK's leading private hospital operators with approximately 38 hospitals nationwide. Our relationship spans both large capital expenditure projects approved by the Group and smaller works funded from individual hospital budgets.

We also work with Spire Healthcare, delivering similar high-quality refurbishment services.
