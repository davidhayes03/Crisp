7 Eden View is a showcase of modern craftsmanship and thoughtful design, forming part of an exclusive private development of seven traditionally styled homes set around a landscaped courtyard in the heart of Edenbridge. This detached two-bedroom residence, completed in 2023, is designed for discerning buyers seeking quality, comfort, and a prime location.


  • Exclusive private courtyard development
  • Detached two-bedroom residence
  • High specification kitchen with integrated appliances
  • Private allocated parking
  • No onward chain
  • Converted circa 2023
  • Energy-efficient UPVC windows
  • Prime town center location

The property is approached via its own private entrance and features a spacious open-plan living, dining, and kitchen area. The high specification kitchen is equipped with integrated oven, hob, dishwasher, and fridge/freezer. A downstairs WC adds convenience, while stairs lead to two generous double bedrooms and a contemporary family bathroom on the first floor.


Finished to an exceptional standard, this home offers stylish and low-maintenance living in the heart of Edenbridge Town Centre. Energy-efficient UPVC windows and allocated parking further enhance comfort and security.


The development is ideally situated within walking distance of Edenbridge High Street, with its historic buildings, independent shops, Waitrose supermarket, and a variety of eateries and public houses. Residents benefit from excellent transport links, including two mainline railway stations with direct services to London Bridge in under 45 minutes, and easy access to Sevenoaks, Oxted, Tunbridge Wells, and East Grinstead.


Surrounded by greenbelt countryside, Edenbridge is rich in history and community spirit, offering a leisure center, prestigious golf clubs, and a selection of outstanding schools. The area is also close to Hever Castle, National Trust attractions, and Bluewater Shopping Centre.
