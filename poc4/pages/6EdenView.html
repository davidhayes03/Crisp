Welcome to 6 Eden View, part of an exclusive private development of seven traditionally designed homes set around a secure, landscaped courtyard in the heart of Edenbridge. This stunning detached two-bedroom residence, converted in 2023, exemplifies our commitment to quality, comfort, and contemporary living.


  • Exclusive private courtyard development
  • Detached two-bedroom residence
  • High specification kitchen with integrated appliances
  • Private allocated parking
  • No onward chain
  • Converted circa 2023
  • Energy-efficient UPVC windows
  • Prime town center location

Designed for modern lifestyles, the property features two spacious double bedrooms, a high specification kitchen with integrated oven, hob, dishwasher, and fridge/freezer, and a bright, open-plan living and dining area. The home benefits from its own private entrance, allocated parking, and UPVC sealed windows throughout, ensuring energy efficiency and security.


Our development is ideally located within walking distance of Edenbridge High Street, renowned for its historic architecture, independent shops, Waitrose supermarket, and vibrant local amenities. Residents enjoy easy access to two mainline railway stations, connecting to London Bridge in under 45 minutes, as well as excellent road links to Sevenoaks, Oxted, Tunbridge Wells, and East Grinstead.


Each home is finished to the highest standard, blending traditional design with modern convenience. The development offers a unique opportunity for buyers seeking a secure, low-maintenance residence with no onward chain, set in a thriving community surrounded by greenbelt countryside, prestigious golf clubs, and outstanding schools.
