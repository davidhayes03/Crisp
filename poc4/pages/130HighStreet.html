130 High Street represents a landmark conversion project in the heart of Edenbridge. This former Star Pub has been thoughtfully transformed into four distinctive residential properties, each offering modern living while preserving the building's historic character.


Immaculate, completely refurbished throughout. This two double bedroom Georgian converted property is just a short walk from Edenbridge High Street and mainline train stations. This charming home retains original features, combining period character with a modern twist. The property also benefits from a secure, sunny garden with rear access—perfect for alfresco dining—backing onto the River Eden and close to open countryside and walks. Offered with no onward chain.

The front door leads into an entrance hall with stairs to the first floor and a door into the reception room. The ground floor includes a lovely sitting room with herringbone flooring throughout the open plan reception room, a window to the front, and glazed double doors onto the garden. The brand new kitchen offers a good range of wall and base units and drawers, including integrated white goods. The ground floor also benefits from a downstairs WC. Upstairs, there are two good sized double bedrooms. Outside, there is a bright, sunny garden—perfect for entertaining friends and family.


The development showcases our commitment to quality craftsmanship and attention to detail. Each of the four houses features bespoke interiors, premium finishes, and carefully designed living spaces that make the most of the building's unique architecture.
